I am a UI Designer with a dedication to creating simplistic and intuitive design.

Shelter Mobile

Shelter Mobile is a mobile application prototype that I created with two other designers. A design alumni from The Iron Yard was our "client" and gave us the details for our project. Our task was to make a platform for foster parents to arrange rides to and from adoption events and clinic visits.

GoodReads

GoodReads is a popular literary website made by Amazon where you can create reading lists, review and discuss titles, and get suggestions for what to read next. This project is a redesign concept for a more organized and user-friendly site.

Rental U

Rental U is a conceptual native-application that I designed in Adobe Illustrator and Pixate. I designed Rental U to act as a virtual apartment locator service that allows the user to find the apartment that is the right fit for them and book tours of these apartments all from the app. This project encompassed a wide range of UI and UX discoveries, planning, and design.

Pocket

Pocket is a popular website with over 15 million users where you can easily save your favorite articles, videos and more for later. I coded a responsive version of Pocket from visually looking at a screenshot of the site.
